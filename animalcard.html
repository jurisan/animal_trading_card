Koi Fish

Koi varieties are distinguished by coloration, patterning, and scalation. Some of the major colors are white, black, red, yellow, blue, and cream

  • Scientific Name: Cyprinus rubrofuscus
  • Average Length: up to about 3.28 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 50 - 200 years
  • Habitat: large ponds

The word "koi" comes from Japanese, simply meaning "carp". It is also a homophone for another word that means "affection" or "love"; koi are therefore symbols of love and friendship in Japan
